# Social Media Advertising Performance Analysis
## Project Overview
Analyzed 300,000 social media advertising campaigns  across Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and Pinterest  to identify what drives conversions and where ad budget is being wasted.

## Business Problem
Why is ad spend not converting into customers?
Which channels, audiences, and segments deliver the best return on investment?

## Tools Used
-Microsoft Excel: data cleaning, CTR, CPC, CPA calculations
-Microsoft Power BI: interactive dashboard and visualizations

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle - Social Media Advertising Dataset
- Size: 300,000 campaigns
- Channels: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest
- Audience Segments: 9 target groups

## Key Findings
- Instagram delivers the highest ROI at 4.01
- Pinterest delivers the lowest ROI at 0.72 - the biggest budget drain
- Men 45-60 have the lowest CPA - most efficient audience
- Men 25-34 have the highest CPA - the least efficient audience
- All channels convert at an identical 0.08% rate

## Key Insight
Conversion rate is uniform across all channels and  audiences, meaning the problem is NOT the ad targeting. The issues are the post-click experience, landing page, checkout process, and mobile responsiveness.

## Recommendations
1. Reduce Pinterest budget,  reallocate to Instagram
2. Prioritize the Men 45-60 audience targeting
3. Audit landing page and checkout experience
4. Focus on the Market Expansion campaign goal
5. Fix the post-click customer journey before increasing spend
